## Security Benefits

Enabling Two-Factor Authentication provides several important security advantages:

- Adds an additional layer of authentication beyond a password.
- Reduces the effectiveness of stolen or leaked passwords.
- Increases resistance against phishing and credential theft attacks.
- Supports defence-in-depth security principles.
- Improves protection of personal and development-related resources stored within GitHub.
