# B21 – Evidence Summary

This activity involved designing and implementing a cybersecurity learning activity using Typeform. The activity focused on phishing awareness and was designed to help users identify common warning signs associated with phishing attacks.

## Learning Activity

An interactive phishing awareness question was created using Typeform. Participants were presented with a realistic phishing email scenario and asked to identify the biggest warning sign indicating that the email may be a phishing attempt.

The activity focused on teaching users how to recognize common phishing indicators, including urgency, requests for sensitive information, and social engineering techniques.

## Activity Outcome

After submitting their answer, participants received feedback explaining the correct response and why the email displayed characteristics commonly associated with phishing attacks.

The activity reinforced the importance of carefully evaluating emails before clicking links or providing sensitive information.

## Reflection

Designing and implementing this cybersecurity learning activity demonstrated how interactive learning tools can improve cybersecurity awareness. The activity encouraged participants to think critically about suspicious emails and recognize common phishing indicators. This exercise highlighted the importance of user education as a key defense against phishing and other social engineering attacks.
